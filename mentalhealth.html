Mental health

Mental health isn’t just about not having depression and anxiety it’s also about making sure you're enjoying life. Mental health affects everyone when they are not happy for a long time. Sometimes mental health can get very bad to the point where you have to take medications for it. Mental health is connected to physical health because when a person is down, they stop going out and taking care of their hygiene. Mental health affects teens more than it does adults.

What is good mental health? Good mental health is being in a safe, secure mindset. Conditions such as stress, anxiety isolation and more, can affect your mental health. Unstable mental health could cause depression, family and friend conflict and more. Anyone is in risk of developing mental health disorders, so it is important that you take care of and prevent this from happening. Stay healthy and Alert.

What can cause Mental Health issues?

There are many things that cause Mental Health for example, social isolation or loneliness, bereavement (losing someone close to you), long-term stress, and having a long-term physical health condition.

What are some solutions to help mental health and why can bad mental health be dangerous?

Since everyone's body, mind and well-being are different, there are many options that can help mental health. Such as, talk to a therapist, psychotherapy (psychotherapy is the therapeutic treatment of mental illness provided by a trained mental health professional), Support Group (a support is a group of people who have problems and the host there can help them). Bad Mental Health can be dangerous because that people can be so depressed or stress that they would want to commit suicide. When this person does this you would have to put them in a mental hospital.
